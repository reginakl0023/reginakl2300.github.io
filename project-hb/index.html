Your Speakers

Tomas Brown Former Vanderbilt Assistant Director of Undergraduate Admissions

Tomas has seven years of college admissions experience, including roles as an admissions officer and school counselor. She earned a master's in higher education from the University of Pennsylvania and worked at Vanderbilt University as an Assistant Director of Admissions for five years. Later, she became the Associate Director of College Counseling at Marist School in Atlanta, managing college admissions support for over 100 students annually. She also worked as a Customer Success Manager for a company providing college admissions guidance systems for high schools.

Alex Rivers Former Cornell Admissions Officer

With 22 years in college admissions, Alex Rivers has reviewed over 20,000 applications at top institutions like Cornell University, Brandeis University, Bates College, and the University of Maine. At Cornell, he served as Assistant Director and Multicultural Recruitment Coordinator. His expertise in athletic admissions and personalized counseling approach helps students find the best college match. Known for his friendly and approachable style, he builds rapport with students and reassures parents.
